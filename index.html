Problem Statement: Assignment organization

There is no time-efficient wat to condense and view important assignments and exam due dates.

Affinity diagram: Scheduling Application

Developing an easy-to-use scheduling application tailored to faculty and students requires a lot of forethought on the developers' end and with the help of an affinity diagram, our team brainstormed potential features and obstacles of development.

Team Personas:[title]

My persona is about a man named John Banner, who is a college athlete and struggles keeping up with all their assignments and Practice. They need a better way to see all their assignments and when there due so they can make it to practice and games on time and also not have to worry and about their grades. John said, "I need a better way to see my assignments on time", and our website does just that.
